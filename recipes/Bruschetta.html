Bruschetta


Description

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer that is easy to make at home. Toasted bread is topped with a mixture of juicy tomatoes, garlic, Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, and balsamic.


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, about 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.
  2. Meanwhile, toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl.
  3. Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.
  4. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices.
